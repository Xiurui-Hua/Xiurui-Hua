## About

I am a graduate student working on 3D scene representation and editing.  
My research mainly focuses on **3D Gaussian Splatting (3DGS)**, especially geometry-aware mask refinement and text-driven 3D editing.

Currently, I am studying how the covariance of 3D Gaussians can be used as a structural prior for improving 3D mask propagation, object deletion, and appearance editing.
